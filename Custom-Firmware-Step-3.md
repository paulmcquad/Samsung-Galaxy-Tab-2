## BackUp/Restore your Current ROM 
 
1. "Backup" or "Restore"
2. "Backup/Restore to sdcard1" (Where SDCard1 is your External SDCard)

## Preparing your Device for the New ROM

Preform a clean wipe

1. "Wipe" > "Swipe to Factory Reset"

## Flashing your New ROM (Android 6.0.1)

1. Install
2. Select: "Install Zip"
3. Select: "Install zip from /storage/sdcard1
4. Select the ROM File. ( i.e: "cm-13.0-20160911-1524-UNOFFICIAL-espressowifi.zip"
5. Confirm and let it complete the operation.

## Flashing GApps

1. Install
2. Select: "Install Zip"
3. Select: "Install zip from /storage/sdcard1
4. Select the ROM File. ( i.e: "A-GAPPS+6.0.1+v5.1-ARM_64.zip" )
5. Confirm and let it complete the operation.


## Perform a Clean Reinstall

Again Preform a clean wipe

1. "Wipe" > "Swipe to Factory Reset"


You're Done.
Enjoy the "New" Android 6.0.1
